Homepage slider photos

These full-screen photos rotate behind the homepage headline. Use wide, landscape photos of your productions — 3 to 5 great ones is perfect.

Gallery photos & videos

Photos and short video clips for the "From the Wings" gallery. The file name becomes the caption — e.g. andha-yug-curtain-call.jpg shows as "Andha yug curtain call". Keep videos under 25 MB.

Cast & crew

The first 4 people appear on the homepage; everyone appears on the Artists page. Drag the order by editing top-to-bottom — the list shows in this order.

Productions / Journey

Shown in the "Our Journey Begins" section on the homepage and on the Productions page. Add every show you stage — this list is your company's growing history.
