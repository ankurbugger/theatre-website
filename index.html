Our Story

A small company with a big stage in its heart

Rangmanch began in a rehearsal room barely bigger than a green room — five actors, one borrowed spotlight, and a conviction that Indian stories deserve Indian stages. Today we produce original writing, classic revivals, and musicals that travel from intimate black-box venues to thousand-seat auditoriums.

We're launching across India first, and taking our productions to international festivals and diaspora audiences next.

  • Original writingNew Indian plays, commissioned every season
  • Rooted & fearlessFolk forms meet contemporary staging
  • Theatre for allTickets priced so no one is left outside
